  • Chapter Three

    The Pandemic

    2020

    Within a year of meeting, Aman moved to Gothenburg and Vika moved to Paris. They thought they would survive just fine as they were only a few hours away. Little did they know, a little little microscopic being had other plans. Their meetings and conversations were suddenly bound to a tiny magical mirror in their hands. Amidst the chaos, they found solace in each other, talking about life, universe and everything else you think of when left alone with your thoughts.

  • Chapter Four

    Growing with each other

    2021

    So my dear readers, do you know what the best part of getting to know someone well is? Sharing your hobbies and growing together. Soon after things started settling down, our little explorers started taking hikes together, doing yoga together and cooking together. While Aman prefers going by the book, Vika prefers going off-the-cuff which keeps their experimental cooking expeditions balanced and poised.
